# Privacy Policy — TransVigo Driver

**Effective date:** 8 June 2026
**App:** TransVigo Driver (`com.transvigo.driver`)
**Provider:** TransVigo

> Host this page at a public HTTPS URL (e.g. `https://transvigo.in/privacy`) and
> paste that URL into the Play Console listing and Data Safety form. Google
> requires the policy to be reachable without logging in.

TransVigo Driver is a workforce app used by drivers and operations staff of a
fleet business to manage trips, log expenses, raise maintenance tickets, and
receive work notifications. Accounts are created by the fleet operator; the app
is not offered to the general public.

## Information we collect

- **Account information** — name, email address and/or phone number, and your
  assigned role. Created and managed by your employer (the fleet operator).
- **Operational data you enter** — trips, fuel and toll entries, maintenance
  tickets, job cards, and related notes.
- **Photos** — receipt, odometer, and ticket photos you choose to capture or
  upload. Used only to document the related record.
- **Location** — approximate and precise location, collected only while you use
  the app, to auto-fill the location of incident/ticket reports. We do not track
  location in the background.
- **Push notification token** — a device identifier issued by the OS push
  service, used to deliver work notifications.

## How we use information

We use the data solely to operate the app's features: assigning and tracking
trips, recording expenses and maintenance, sending work-related notifications,
and providing the fleet operator with operational reporting. We do **not** use
your data for advertising, and we do **not** sell personal data.

## Sharing

Data is shared only with your employing fleet operator and the service providers
that host the app's backend (database, file storage, and push-notification
delivery). We do not share data with third parties for their own purposes.

## Data retention

Operational records are retained for as long as your employer maintains its
TransVigo account, then deleted or anonymised in line with their instructions.

## Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on access-controlled infrastructure.
Credentials are stored encrypted on the device using the platform secure store.

## Your choices

- Camera, location, and notification permissions can be revoked at any time in
  your device settings; related features will be disabled.
- To access or delete your account data, contact your fleet operator or us at
  the address below.

## Contact

Questions or data requests: **support@transvigo.in**
